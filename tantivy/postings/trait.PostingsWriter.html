The PostingsWriter is in charge of receiving documenting and building a Segment in anonymous memory.

PostingsWriter writes in a Heap.

Required Methods

fn suscribe(&mut self, doc: DocId, pos: u32, term: &Term, heap: &Heap)

Record that a document contains a term at a given position.

  • doc - the document id
  • pos - the term position (expressed in tokens)
  • term - the term
  • heap - heap used to store the postings informations as well as the terms in the hashmap.

fn serialize(&self,
             serializer: &mut PostingsSerializer,
             heap: &Heap)
             -> Result<()>

Serializes the postings on disk. The actual serialization format is handled by the PostingsSerializer.

fn close(&mut self, heap: &Heap)

Closes all of the currently open Recorder's.
